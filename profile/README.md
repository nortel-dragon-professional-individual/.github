<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>Dragon Professional Individual is a speech recognition solution designed to let professionals dictate documents, control their computer and transcribe audio by voice - reducing typing and manual input.</p>

<h2>Main Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Real-time dictation with up to 99% recognition accuracy out of the box.</li>
  <li>Deep Learning speech engine adapts to your voice, accent, and environmental noise.</li>
  <li>Text formatting and editing by voice: you can insert punctuation, format text (bold, underline, capitalization), apply Smart Format Rules for dates, numbers, phone numbers etc.</li>
  <li>Custom vocabulary and custom commands: you can add industry-specific terms, acronyms, proper names; create voice commands or auto-text/macro commands to insert frequently used text or graphics.</li>
  <li>Transcription of prerecorded audio: supports common audio formats (.wav, .mp3, .wma, .dss, .ds2, .m4a) for transcribing voice notes, interviews or podcasts.</li>
  <li>Voice-control of computer: open applications, navigate menus, send emails, search web or desktop - full voice control instead of keyboard and mouse.</li>
  <li>Text-to-speech playback: Dragon can read back your dictated text to aid proofreading.</li>
</ul>
